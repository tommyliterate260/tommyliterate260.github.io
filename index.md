HcqDrive turns your Android phone into a private file storage system for your home network. You maintain full control over your data. You do not need external cloud services or company accounts. You move files between your phone and your computer using your existing Wi-Fi connection.

## 📋 How It Works

This software runs a file server on your phone. You scan a QR code on your computer screen to connect devices. Your files stay on your local network. No data leaves your home.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If you cannot connect your devices, check these common items:

- **Network Isolation:** Some public or enterprise Wi-Fi networks block device communication. Use a private home network for the best results.
- **Firewall Settings:** Ensure your Windows firewall allows HcqDrive to communicate on your local network. You may see a prompt during the first launch; select "Allow Access" to proceed.
- **Signal Strength:** Move your phone closer to your wireless router if transfers seem slow or if the connection drops.
- **Permission Denied:** If the app cannot see your files, go to your phone settings, find HcqDrive under Apps, and verify that Storage permissions are set to "Allow."

## 🔒 Data Privacy

HcqDrive operates on a zero-knowledge principle. Your files never pass through a central server or third-party cloud. The communication happens strictly between your phone and your computer. Your account information is not required, as the software does not track usage or maintain user profiles. 

## 📝 Frequently Asked Questions

**Will I lose my files if my Wi-Fi goes out?**
Files remain on your phone's internal storage and your computer's hard drive. The connection depends on your network, but your saved data remains safe on your devices at all times.

**Can I share files with friends outside my house?**
HcqDrive works over your local area network (LAN). It does not provide remote access over the internet. This design keeps your files private and secure from external threats.

**Do I need an internet connection?**
You need an internet connection to initially download the software. Once installed, you only need your local Wi-Fi router to transmit files.

**How many devices can I connect?**
You can link multiple computers to one phone at the same time, provided they are all on the same network segment.

**Does the app drain my phone battery?**
The app consumes power while the server runs. Close the app on your phone when you finish your session to conserve battery life.
